Q2: Which change do you think will have the biggest impact on your focus and well-being? Why?
Answer: Getting an ergonomic mousepad will have the biggest immediate impact. While auditing the rest of my setup, I realized my wrists are actively hurting. Adjusting desk height is helpful, but adding dedicated wrist support addresses an active source of physical friction. Alleviating that pain will prevent long-term strain and allow me to sustain focus during long development sessions without physical distraction.

Q3: How is organizing your digital workspace different from organizing your physical workspace? What parallels do you notice?
Answer: Organizing a physical workspace is primarily a matter of spatial management—if you have the physical space, you can usually store items away neatly. Digital organization is fundamentally different because while it's incredibly fast to "clean up" by moving files into folders, it is significantly easier to forget exactly where you stored those digital items compared to physical objects. The parallel between the two is that both types of clutter create friction: physical clutter limits your movement and distracts the eye, while mismanaged digital clutter drains time and mental energy when searching for lost resources.
